How Brick Sizes

Let your imagination soar! While brick may seem simple, you’re not limited to standard sizes. Bricks Land Company offers a wide variety of sizes and shapes, ensuring you can find the perfect option to add distinctive character to your project. Use our dimensions guide below to select the face brick, structural brick, or clay paver that meets your specifications. To estimate the savings you could achieve by using our oversized brick on your next project, be sure to check out our Oversized Brick Cost Calculator.
