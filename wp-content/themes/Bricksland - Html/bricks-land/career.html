Why Test Result

As part of our quality control procedures, The Belden Brick Company conducts tests on all brick bodies (shale, fireclay, and shale and fireclay combinations) from each plant every 24 months to determine compliance with established construction standards. In the case where testing during a production run is requested, the customer will bear the costs of testing unless the brick does not conform to the requirements of the appropriate specifications. The cost of the test then shall be borne by The Belden Brick Company. Efflorescence is omitted from the results below since all test results are negative.
